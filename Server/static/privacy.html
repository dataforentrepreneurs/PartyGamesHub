Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

Welcome to PartyGames Hub. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

PartyGames Hub is designed to be privacy-friendly. We do not require account registration or collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or phone number to play.

  • Gameplay Data: We may collect anonymous gameplay statistics (e.g., room creation, game completion) to improve our service.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic information about your TV or mobile device (model, OS version) to ensure compatibility.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected anonymous data to:

  • Monitor for stability and technical errors via Sentry.
  • Analyze usage patterns via PostHog to enhance the user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share anonymous information with trusted third-party services for technical stability (Sentry) and usage analytics (PostHog).

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of our systems and the data we process.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
